About Me

My name is Anik Dey and I am an aspiring magician that lives in the metro Atlanta area in Georgia. I am currently in high school and I have many hobbies. I play the violin, I draw and paint, and I program. Sometimes, I write poems and short stories. Magic is my passion, so that is why I decided to pursue this interest. I wanted to start a business in the metro Atlanta area where I perform magic tricks for 8 year olds and above. I perform tricks that are not meant for very little kids as they are professional. As you have heard, 50% of the money goes to charity so you will be doing a huge favor for society by booking us. We take a 25% advance during the booking, and the rest of the payment will be made after the show. You can write a check to us for the payment. I do all sorts of tricks during the show that can entertain the audience for almost an hour.

Book us today for an amazing and entertaining show with 15+ tricks!
